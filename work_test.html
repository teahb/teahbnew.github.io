DIAL UP Store


An exercise in web design and development


summer 2018

The DIAL UP Store was published online in early July 2018 after a year-long period of selling DIAL UP merchandise through word of mouth and at events. As the project's 'owner,' I revitalized our merchandise designs, built relationships with local printing presses, managed logistical operations for order fulfillment, oversaw the photoshoot at Camp Greenway, TN (the horses were already there), and designed and developed the store site.
