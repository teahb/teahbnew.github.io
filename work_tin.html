Tin Handle


Dimension_ Industrial Dsgn [Concept]

Context_ Capstone Dsgn Class [Solo]

Season_ Winter [2017]

Tin is a door handle designed to make full use of a used tin can. It was designed with my personal design aesthetic in mind, coupled with the Bauhaus design philosophy of intentionality, functionality, and symmetry. I named this concept “Deliberate Renewal.”



The top and bottom of the tin can is sliced off, with the top used as the trim plate, and the can itself sliced and rolled into an outside lever. The remaining bottom piece attaches the lever to the trim plate. A leather strip, fastened to the lever, adds comfort and appeal.

[Concept]

"For this project, I chose to explore the concept of deliberate renewal."
