Navigate the World


Dimension_UI Dsgn

Context_Digital Interactions Class [Team of 3_Wireframe Designer/"Developer"]

Season_Fall [2016]

Adjusting to a foreign culture is not always an issue for American students studying abroad, but participating in that culture can prove a challenge.


Navigate the World takes two forms, as a mobile application and Snapchat Discover story, to explore how to better engage this niche audience in cultural participation. In app form, Navigate offers students a search tool to find activities in their locality and price range. In Snapchat form, Navigate offers a news experience "story" that can be both independently consumed and shared with friends.
