the thing


I did production design (costumes and set) for our 'Riverside High' music video in the winter of 2018. The video portrays the after-party of a high school football rally, and so the set and costumes were designed to look like high schoolers orchestrated the whole thing. There was a lot of ripping t-shirts, messy paint, and flying streamers involved.


Official DIAL UP premiere of 'Riverside High' coming soon. In the meantime, listen to the song on DIAL UP CD (Side A).
