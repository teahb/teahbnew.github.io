teah khadijeh brands

studies Economics and Design at Northwestern University.

In other words, she's trying to figure out why people make the choices they do, and how to make their options better.

Teah is intrigued by how people navigate space - both virtually as well as IRL. In her spare time, she thinks about clothes, buys ice cream, eats ice cream, wonders if she should post more on Instagram...and works on DIAL UP.
