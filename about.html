Teah Khadijeh Brands makes space.

Teah is a Brooklyn-based designer earning her MFA in Interiors at Parsons. Her work centers unmasking and destabilizing anti-Black racism in space. She graduated from Northwestern University in 2018 with a Bachelor's degree in Economics and Design — a combination she sees as the study of how people make choices and how we can create better options. She envisions a career ahead in theorizing, imagining, and practicing methods of inclusive design.

In her spare time, Teah watches crime documentaries, re-organizes her furniture, collages, admires the creations of her peers, adds to her Are.na, eats Haagen-Dazs, and very occasionally posts on Instagram.
