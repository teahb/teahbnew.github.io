ABOUT WORK

TEAH (KHADIJEH BRANDS)

studies Economics and Design at Northwestern University.

In other words, she's trying to figure out why people make the choices they do, and how to make their options better.

Teah is intrigued by human behavior and how people navigate space. In her spare time, she thinks about clothes, buys ice cream, eats ice cream, wonders if she should post more on Instagram...and works on DIAL UP.
