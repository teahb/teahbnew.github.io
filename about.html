about work

teah khadijeh brands

is intrigued by how people navigate space - virtually and IRL.

Teah is a Project Manager at NYC-based architecture & interiors firm Martell-Donagher. She graduated from Northwestern University in June 2018 with a Bachelor's degree in Economics and Design — a combination she sees as the study of how people make choices and how we can create better options.

In her spare time, she window shops, works in SketchUp, DJs in Bushwick, eats ice cream, contemplates posting on Instagram...and works on DIAL UP.
