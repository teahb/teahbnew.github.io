Tin

Studying how household materials might be reused, Designing Tin.


Product Design


Winter 2017


Tin is a door handle designed to make full use of a used tin can. It was designed with my personal design aesthetic in mind, coupled with the Bauhaus design philosophy of intentionality, functionality, and symmetry. I named this concept “Deliberate Renewal.”



Deliberate Renewal in reflected in the way that the tin can is re-imagined into the door handle. The tin is partially deconstructed, and then given new purpose in its design. The top and bottom metal is sliced off, with the top used as the trim plate, and the can itself sliced and rolled into an outside lever. The remaining bottom piece attaches the lever to the trim plate. A leather strip, fastened to the lever, adds comfort and appeal.
